# Complete System Implementation Prompt

## System Overview

Build a comprehensive College Management System that handles financial tracking, salary management, attendance monitoring, and complete student history management.

---

## Core Requirements

### 1. Financial Management

#### Daily Financial Tracking:
- **Daily Income:**
  - Track amount of money received each day
  - Record all income sources (student fees, donations, etc.)
  - Breakdown by payment method, department, and student
  - Generate daily income reports
  
- **Daily Expenses:**
  - Track all expenses incurred each day
  - Categorize expenses (operational, utilities, supplies, etc.)
  - Record vendor details and payment methods
  - Generate daily expense reports
  
- **Daily Summary:**
  - Calculate net balance (income - expenses)
  - Provide department-wise breakdown
  - Visual charts and graphs

#### Monthly Financial Management:
- **Monthly Income:**
  - Sum of all daily incomes in the month
  - Income source analysis
  - Payment method trends
  - Month-over-month comparison
  
- **Monthly Expenses:**
  - Total monthly expenses including:
    - Operational expenses
    - Teacher salary payments
    - Staff salary payments
    - Department expenses
    - Other expenses
  - Category-wise breakdown
  - Department-wise breakdown
  
- **Monthly Financial Summary:**
  - Total monthly income
  - Total monthly expenses (including all salaries)
  - Net monthly balance
  - Profit/loss statement
  - Visual reports and analytics

#### Reporting Periods:
- **Daily Reports:** Income, expenses, and net balance for each day
- **Weekly Reports:** 7-day summaries with comparisons
- **Monthly Reports:** Complete monthly financial statements
- **Yearly Reports:** Annual summaries and year-over-year analysis
- **Custom Date Range:** Generate reports for any selected period

### 2. Teacher Salary Management

#### Monthly Salary Payments:
- Record monthly salary payments to all teachers
- Track for each teacher:
  - Teacher ID, name, department, designation
  - Base salary, allowances, deductions
  - Net salary amount
  - Payment date and method
  - Payment status (Paid, Pending, Partial)
  
#### Salary Features:
- Automatic salary calculation
- Generate salary slips
- Track payment history
- Department-wise salary reports
- Total monthly salary expenditure
- Salary payment trends and alerts

### 3. Attendance Management

#### Student Attendance:
- Track daily attendance for all students
- Record status: **Present** or **Absent**
- Additional options: Late, Excused
- Features:
  - Daily attendance marking
  - Bulk attendance entry
  - Individual student marking
  - Attendance history tracking
  - Attendance percentage calculation
  - Absenteeism reports

#### Teacher Attendance:
- Track daily attendance for all teachers
- Record status: **Present**, **Absent**, or **On Leave**
- Additional options: Late
- Features:
  - Daily attendance marking
  - Leave management
  - Attendance history
  - Attendance percentage
  - Leave balance tracking

#### Attendance Reports:
- Daily attendance sheets
- Student-wise attendance history
- Teacher-wise attendance history
- Department-wise attendance
- Monthly attendance summaries
- Attendance trends and analytics

### 4. Student History Management

#### Comprehensive Student Records:
- Maintain complete history of all students in the college, academy, or school
- Include:
  - Admission information and documents
  - Academic history (courses, performance, results)
  - Financial history (all fee payments, receipts)
  - Attendance history (complete record)
  - Personal information updates
  - Document history
  - Status changes

#### Data Organization:
- **By Department:** Organize students by their department
- **By Course Category:** Group by course/category
- **By Admission Year:** Organize by batch/year
- **By Status:** Active, Inactive, Graduated, etc.

#### Data Accessibility:
- **Search Functionality:**
  - Primary: Search by serial number
  - Additional: Search by name, department, course, year
  - Advanced filters and sorting
  
- **Quick Access:**
  - Recent records
  - Frequently accessed
  - Quick navigation
  
- **Data Export:**
  - Export complete student records
  - Export specific data sets
  - Generate comprehensive reports
  - PDF and Excel export

#### Historical Data Preservation:
- Maintain all historical records
- Archive instead of delete
- Long-term data storage
- Backup and recovery
- Data integrity and audit trails
